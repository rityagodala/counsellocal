# CounselLocal

**AI legal assistant for solo and small firm attorneys in North Georgia.**

🌐 **Live:** https://counsellocal.vercel.app

---

## What it is

CounselLocal drafts Georgia-specific legal documents in seconds — divorce petitions, motions to suppress, wills — for the 40+ solo and small firms in Forsyth County who are still using Word templates from 2009.

Harvey is for BigLaw. General Legal is for VC-backed founders. CounselLocal is for the attorney handling a custody hearing in Cumming, GA on a Tuesday morning.

## Who it's for

- Solo practitioners in Forsyth, Cherokee, and Dawson County, GA
- Small firms (1–5 attorneys) doing family law, criminal defense, estate planning, real estate

## Pricing (planned)
